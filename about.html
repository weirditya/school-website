About Greenfield Public School

Building character, competence, and confidence since 1995.

Our Vision & Mission

At Greenfield Public School, our mission is to foster holistic development in students through a balanced blend of academics, sports, and creativity. We aim to nurture responsible citizens who value integrity, empathy, and lifelong learning.

Principal’s Message

“Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire.” At Greenfield Public School, we believe in empowering our students to think critically, act responsibly, and dream fearlessly. Together, we create an environment of mutual respect and curiosity, where every child’s potential is celebrated.

– Mrs. Kavita Sharma, Principal
